## About me

I am a PhD student in the team of Alain Vanderpoorten. I obtained a FRIA doctoral grant in 2021 for the duration of 4 years. My project, which is a follow-up on my master’s thesis, focuses on the study of the geographical distribution of land plants along ecological gradients. This is done through the analysis of their phylogenetic relationship which enables me to determine which processes could have produced the observed distribution patterns. I am particularly interested in the phylogenetic turnover aspect of the study, which will first be tested on simulated data, and subsequently applied on a real dataset sampled within the framework of a larger Swiss project titled GEN4MIG.
