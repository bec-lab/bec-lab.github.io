Lea Mouton

## About me My name is Lea Mouton. I am assistant to Nicolas Magain in the Evolution and Conservation Biology Unit at the University of Liège, which is part of the InBioS Research Center and the Department of Biology, Ecology and Evolution (BEE). I am also doing a PhD with Alain Vanderpoorten about the drivers and the variation of Beta diversity with scales and taxa. ### My work After presenting my master's thesis on horizontal and vertical variation of Beta diversity in epiphytic pteridophyte communities in subtropical forest of China, I was interested to continue working on Beta diversity for a PhD. I first focused my work on the drivers and variations of Beta diversity with spatial scale among land plant taxa in Macaronesia. Currently, I am working on the variation of Walloon bryophyte communities in time and space. I also try to identify the pollutants or pesticides that has the most impact on their community using Beta diversity and GDM (generalized dissimilarity model).
