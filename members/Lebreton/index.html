Elise Lebreton

    About me

    I am a FRIA PhD student. In the context of my thesis project, I study the systematics and ecology of lichens growing on tree leaves (=foliicolous lichens) in tropical environments. My work also focuses on the biodiversity of lichens in the French overseas territories. My previous work at the National Museum of Natural History in Paris (ISYEB department), focused on the endemic flora (Spermatophytes, Pteridophytes, Bryophytes) of the French overseas territories: FEnTOM project. As part of my university training (master BioGET, University of Montpellier, France) I studied tropical ecosystems but also carried out several works on the digitalization of herbaria: Montpellier herbarium (MPU) and Paris herbarium (P and PC).
