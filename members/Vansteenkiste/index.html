Arnaud Vansteenkiste

    About me

    Arnaud is a researcher working on the mortality of the Red Kite (Milvus milvus) by windmills. This project is funded by SPW (Wallonia public administration). The three main goals of the project are:

    1. Measure mortality via a search for corpses on the field in two windmill sites in Wallonia

    2. Infer the viability of the population based on various growth scenarios in a statistical framework

    3. From an ethics standpoint, study what an acceptable mortality is to justify a good conservation status
