Antoine Simon

    About me

    Antoine successfully defended his PhD thesis entitled 'The multi-organismal nature of lobarioid lichens: phylogenetic and transcriptomic studies on photomorphs' in October 2020. He then pursued with a BAEF post-doctoral fellow at UConn in Bernard Goffinet's lab (2021-2022), then got awarded a Marie Skłodowska-Curie postdoctoral fellowship to work with Prof. Einar Timdal (Natural History Museum, University of Oslo) on genomic approaches applied to historical herbarium specimens (2022-2024). He is now a reasearcher at the Swedish Museum of Natural History.
